About OpenTCAD

The Problem

Semiconductor process simulation is essential for device research and education, but commercial TCAD tools are expensive, slow, and difficult to set up. Most university labs cannot afford licenses, and students rarely get hands-on experience during their coursework.

Our Approach

OpenTCAD takes a different path. Instead of solving partial differential equations like traditional TCAD, we use a voxel-based emulation engine that produces 3D process results in minutes.
