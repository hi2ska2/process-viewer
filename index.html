How It Works

From Recipe to 3D Structure in Seconds

Submit your process recipe, and our voxel-based emulator generates step-by-step 3D device structures ‚Äî viewable instantly in your browser.

STEP 01

Input Process Recipe

Define your fabrication steps ‚Äî oxidation, implantation, etching, deposition ‚Äî with parameters via web GUI or YAML.

STEP 02

Cloud Emulation

Our voxel-based engine computes the 3D structure for each process step in seconds on cloud servers.

STEP 03

3D Visualization

Explore the result interactively ‚Äî rotate, zoom, cross-section, compare steps ‚Äî all in your browser via WebGL.

STEP 04

Download & Share

Export structure data as JSON, snapshots as PNG. Share results with colleagues via URL.
